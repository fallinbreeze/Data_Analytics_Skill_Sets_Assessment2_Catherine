Q5 Has the per-person solar capacity gap between Summer and Winter widened from 2011 to 2021??¶

- Objective:¶

Compare per-person solar capacity between Summer and Winter to assess energy reliability.¶

- Key Finding:¶

While overall capacity shows a strong upward trend, the seasonal output remains volatile. Specifically, post-2017 data reveals an increasing divergence between seasons, despite the anomalous peak in Winter 2020..¶

- Business Insight:¶

While both Summer and Winter capacities show exponential growth post-2017, the frequent crossovers and fluctuations between seasons highlight the inherent instability of solar output. This trend emphasizes that capacity growth alone is not enough.¶

To ensure reliable energy year-round, Australia must invest in battery storage to buffer these unpredictable seasonal swings.¶
